Hex Mini is the control app for the same called drone project. Easiest way to fly your tiny drone. It connects to Hex Mini drone via Bluetooth 4.0, has the remote control ability in 60 meters distance.

    Always keep the Hex Mini in sight. Avoid flying the Hex Mini close to young children, domestic animals or fragile objects. Always keep a minimum distance of about 80 inches (2 meters) between the spinning propellers and people, pets, or other objects. Some children may require adult assistance when navigating the activities. Do not expose the battery to heat or dispose of a fire.
